Rooms to Roll Dice in

Create a Room

In order to get a room in which you can roll dice together with your roll playing game, just click on one of the buttons. You will then be rdirected to your newly created room. Just share the URL of that room with your group.

Rooms are automatically deleted if there is no activity for four hours.
